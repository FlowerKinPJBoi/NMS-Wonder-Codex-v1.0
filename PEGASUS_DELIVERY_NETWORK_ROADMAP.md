# Pegasus Delivery Network — Public Roadmap Framework

## Network lanes

- **Pegasus Transit** — verified portal, system, rendezvous, and operator travel routes.
- **Pegasus Courier** — human-operated multiplayer handoff research for transferable items and creature eggs.
- **Pegasus Acquisition** — guided claiming of starships, freighters, frigates, multitools, and natural creatures.
- **Pegasus Dispatch** — future request queue, operator assignment, scheduling, status, audit, and delivery confirmation.

## Importer privacy boundary

The private importer beta may inspect supported saves locally and produce normalized asset previews. Raw saves remain on the user's computer. New Pegasus categories are not uploaded during the beta. Inventory quantities and player-assigned asset names require explicit opt-in, and normalized manifests exclude local file paths, platform account identifiers, and inventory slot coordinates.

## Stages

1. Read-only local collection beta.
2. Capability matrix and controlled human test plans.
3. Backend asset schema and pending review queue.
4. Searchable public asset sections.
5. Human-operated Pegasus pilots.
6. Assisted automation only for workflows proven safe and repeatable.
