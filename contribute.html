SCREENSHOT FIELD GUIDE

Help reviewers see the actual Wonder.

  • Center the subject and keep the entire body, plant, or mineral visible.
  • Use daylight or strong neutral lighting so colors can be judged.
  • Choose a background with clear contrast.
  • For fauna, capture a side or three-quarter view and avoid motion blur.
  • For flora and minerals, include the full object plus one useful detail angle.
  • Upload the original-resolution screenshot rather than a chat-compressed copy.
  • Include a scale or environment view when size is important.

Avoid

  • Scanner tint, menus, large HUD overlays, notifications, or weapon effects.
  • Cropped limbs, leaves, or mineral edges.
  • Night shots where the true color is unclear.
  • Watermarks, decorative text, borders, filters, or AI enhancement.
