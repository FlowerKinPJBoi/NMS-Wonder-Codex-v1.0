CURRENT RESEARCH

What the evidence supports today.

Confirmed findings are reproducible. Strong findings have controlled experimental support. Open questions remain clearly labeled.

Confirmed

Animal projector encoding

Animal DiscoveryData can produce repeatable Wonder Projector Message IDs using UA and the encoded VP sequence.

Confirmed

Flora projector encoding

Flora records reconstruct from UA, VP0, and VP1. Controlled tests separated visual variation from broad family changes.

Mineral projector encoding

Mineral DiscoveryData follows the compact two-VP projector structure and can be normalized by the importer.
